Our Story

Crafting Community

Artisan Blend began as a dream in 2010 when two coffee enthusiasts, Sarah and James, set out to create a space where exceptional coffee and community could blend seamlessly. What started as a small corner cafe has grown into Nairobi's beloved brunch destination.

We're more than just a cafe. We're a hub of warmth, creativity, and exceptional flavors. Every cup we brew and every dish we serve tells a story of passion and dedication to the craft.
